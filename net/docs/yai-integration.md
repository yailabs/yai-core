# YAI Integration

YAI controls authority.
NET moves streams.
External nodes execute capabilities.

YAI may later invoke capability nodes through NET after authority, policy and
operator boundaries have permitted the operation. NET reports transport facts:
node health, endpoint status, route inputs, stream state, transport metrics and
receipt transport state.

NET facts can inform YAI decisions. They do not decide YAI decisions.

NET.SPINE.0 adds no runtime integration and no command behavior.
