  authority.

  policy boundaries.

- Records remain truth. Derived facts, reports and model summaries are not
  truth.

## Residency Contract Proxy

The base frame is intended to represent reusable case material. It may be
logically reused only when case, model, runner, policy, redaction, retrieval,
fact and graph generations remain compatible.
